Support

ChairGun Pro was originally developed by an optics manufacturer in the United Kingdom. Having abandoned it in 2018, over the course of time, it stopped working on many modern devices and operating systems.

In an attempt to get it working on his new device, a user in the Colonies reverse-engineered the program. He succeeded in getting it to run on MacOS Catalina, Windows 10, and he was able to remove enough of the operating system specific calls to also get it to run on Linux – something that it was not at all designed to do. Theoretically, it may now also be very close to being able to be run on tablets.

He contacted the Original Developer about it. They very graciously granted him permission to release his updated program as a “User Supported Edition” without the source code.

This is a User’s attempt to resurrect it.

The Original Developer does not want to be contacted about this software, and indeed, they have no way of supporting it, as they do not even have access to the updated source code for it.

In fact, in an attempt to distance themselves from the project, they have asked this user to remove all references to their company from the program, except for their scopes and reticles, and have even suggested that he add scopes and reticles from other manufacturers.

They have since given him permission to release it with just their scopes, and with their original User Manual.

The best place to obtain answers to questions about how to use ChairGun is the User Manual. This can be found by selecting Help –> Help Topics from the ChairGun menu. It may also be found in your User/ChairGun4/Etc directory.

Questions about installing it, or about any other topic not covered in the user manual should be emailed to the address below.

For questions or support for ChairGun USE, please email:

chairgun.use@hotmail.com

Please do not ask the Original Developer for their source code. And please do not ask the developer of ChairGun USE for his source code, either. He will not release it to anybody, as stipulated in his agreement with them.

And again, please do not contact the Original Developer about this software. They reluctantly gave the developer of ChairGun USE permission to release it. ChairGun in general has obviously been a big disappointment for them.

It is a sore subject with them.

Please leave them at peace about it.
